HEALTH INSURANCE COVERAGE RULES
Standard Policy Terms — v4.1
 
SECTION 1: ELIGIBLE TREATMENTS
1.1 Inpatient hospitalisation for more than 24 hours is covered.
1.2 Day care procedures listed in Schedule B are covered.
1.3 Pre and post hospitalisation expenses covered for 30 days before and 60 days after.
 
SECTION 2: EXCLUSIONS
2.1 Cosmetic or aesthetic treatments are not covered.
2.2 Self-inflicted injuries are not covered.
2.3 Dental treatment unless caused by accident is not covered.
2.4 Experimental treatments not approved by medical authorities are not covered.
2.5 Treatment for pre-existing conditions within the first 2 years is not covered.
 
SECTION 3: CLAIM SUBMISSION TIMELINE
3.1 Cashless claims must be pre-authorised at least 48 hours before planned admission.
3.2 Emergency cashless claims must be notified within 24 hours of admission.
3.3 Reimbursement claims must be submitted within 90 days of discharge.
3.4 Documents submitted beyond 90 days will be rejected unless an extension is approved.
