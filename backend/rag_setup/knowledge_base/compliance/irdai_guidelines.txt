IRDAI DOCUMENT STANDARDS FOR INSURANCE CLAIMS
Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India
Circular Ref: IRDA/HLT/REG/CIR/2024/001
 
1. All claim documents must be in English or include a certified translation.
2. Medical records must be original or certified true copies.
3. Doctor signatures must be accompanied by the doctor's MCI registration number.
4. Hospital bills must be on official hospital letterhead with GST number.
5. All supporting documents must be submitted within the stipulated timeline.
6. Claimants must provide government-issued photo ID for identity verification.
7. Bank account details for NEFT payment must be provided for reimbursement claims.
8. Third-party claims require a First Information Report (FIR) from the police.
